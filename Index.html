About LIT collections

We're dedicated to bringing you the latest trends and timeless classics in sneaker-culture.



As Lit Collections we are at the forefront of footwear innovation in Kenya, with stylish designs. Our mission is to provide our customers with the most comfortable, durable, and fashionable sneakers and footwear on the market.

Founded in 2016, we have quickly become a leader in the nation's footwear industry. Our main goal from then, still being to provide substantial quality to our prestidged customers in and outside Kenya. From sneakers to high-value cotton t-shirts that match the aesthetic.
